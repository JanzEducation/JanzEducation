About Janz Education

Empowering Students Across Rajasthan

Welcome to Janz Education, a dedicated online learning portal designed exclusively for students preparing for board and competitive examinations.

🎯 Our Mission

Our core objective is simple: to provide high-quality, accessible, and syllabus-oriented study materials. We offer Free Mock Tests, detailed test series, and timely updates for exams like the Rajasthan Board (RBSE), Lab Assistant, REET, and BSTC. We believe the right guidance is the ultimate key to success.

👨‍💻 The Founder

Janz Education is independently founded and operated by Mohammad Ayub Janz. As a student and web developer from Vadun Nadi Bhata (BALOTRA) , Rajasthan, the founder understands the exact challenges aspirants face. This platform was built to bridge the gap between students and reliable, free educational resources.
