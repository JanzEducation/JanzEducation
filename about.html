About Janz Education

Empowering Students in Rajasthan

Welcome to Janz Education, a premium online learning portal dedicated exclusively to students preparing for board and competitive examinations across Rajasthan.

🎯 Our Mission

Our core objective is to provide accessible, high-quality, and syllabus-oriented study materials to aspirants. We specialize in offering Free Mock Tests, detailed test series, and timely educational updates for examinations such as the Rajasthan Board (RBSE), Lab Assistant, REET, BSTC, and various other state-level competitive exams. We believe that right guidance and consistent practice are the true keys to success.

👨‍💻 The Founder

Janz Education is independently founded, managed, and operated by Mohammad Ayub Janz. With a deep understanding of the educational landscape and the exact challenges faced by students in Rajasthan, the founder established this platform to bridge the gap between aspirants and reliable study resources.
