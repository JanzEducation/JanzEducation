Welcome to Janz Education, a premium online learning platform dedicated exclusively to students preparing for board and competitive examinations in Rajasthan.

🎯 Our Mission

Our core objective is to provide accessible, high-quality, and syllabus-oriented study materials. We specialize in offering Free Mock Tests, test series, and updates for examinations such as the Rajasthan Board (RBSE), Lab Assistant, REET, BSTC, and various other state-level competitive exams.

👨‍💻 The Founder

Janz Education is independently founded, managed, and operated by Mohammad Ayub Janz. With a deep understanding of the educational landscape and the challenges faced by students in Rajasthan, the founder established this platform to bridge the gap between aspirants and reliable study resources.
